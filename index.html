I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at the Faculty of Legal, Economic and Social Sciences, Mohammedia, affiliated with the Laboratory of Economic Performance and Logistics.

My research focuses on monetary policy, inflation dynamics, central bank independence, macroeconomic stability, and the Moroccan economic environment.

I use econometric modeling and empirical analysis with tools such as EViews, STATA, Python, and R Studio.

Research

My current work studies monetary policy transmission mechanisms, central bank responses to economic shocks, inflation control, and the role of legal and de facto central bank independence in strengthening policy credibility.
